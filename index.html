Pebble Watchface - TJ Schedule

A Pebble Watchface designed to display the daily schedule for TJHSST

View on GitHub Download .zip Download .tar.gz

Download the latest public app! If you have an OG Pebble, Pebble Steel, or even a Pebble Time, the app is compatible with your device! https://apps.getpebble.com/applications/5611bb419af9900b3900004c

Convenient

Easily displays the TJHSST Schedule to the user, alongside pertinent information such as the time and the weather (future). If you don't know what TJHSST, this app probably isn't for you, but here's a link to help you figure it out: www.tjhsst.edu
